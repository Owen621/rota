Info

Step one

Go to add employees, and fill in the form for each employee that you want to be on the rota.

Step two

Go to either generate or custom, or both, and create a rota. Once you are happy with it publish it. Your employees can then login using their provided username and password. They will be taken to a page where they can view the rota.

Step three

Go to employee information to update employee information or remove employees. You will also be able to view employee costs for the week on this page. You can also print the current rota as a pdf to send to your employees.
